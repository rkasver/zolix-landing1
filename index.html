How quickly can I see results? +

Most clients see their personalized savings report within 24 hours of signing up. Full optimization implementation typically completes in 72 hours, and cost reductions appear in your next billing cycle. Our AI continuously monitors and optimizes your infrastructure 24/7.

Is there any downtime during recommendation generation? +

Zero downtime. ZOLIX AI operates with read-only access to analyze your cloud infrastructure and generate recommendations. We don't make any changes to your systems—we simply identify optimization opportunities. Your services remain fully operational throughout the entire analysis process.

We support all major cloud providers: AWS (Amazon Web Services), Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform (GCP), and Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI). Our platform provides unified optimization across your entire multi-cloud environment from a single dashboard.
